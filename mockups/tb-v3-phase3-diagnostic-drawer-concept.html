How a user reads this · the completion pill stays at 5 TO GO on the right rail. Click it → this drawer slides in from the right (translateX(100%) → 0 over 220ms, custom ease-out). Each failure row is a button: click a row → the corresponding device gets selected on the canvas, the drawer stays open, the Inspector opens for editing in parallel. As the user fixes issues, rows fade out (200ms opacity) and the count on the pill decrements. When the last failure resolves, the pill flips to GOALS MET + accent fill, and the drawer empty-state takes over (see § 02 below).
