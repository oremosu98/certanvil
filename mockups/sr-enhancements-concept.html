In Settings

The setup

A few controls, grouped under Daily Review so they read clearly as review settings, decide how big each session feels and how it behaves when the queue is light.

8
Daily Review · Spaced Repetition A labelled group inside the existing Settings page. These only affect your review sessions, separate from the standard quiz and exam setup.
Exam dateDrives the pace (enhancement 7). Clear it any time to go open-ended. Tue 27 May · change
Session sizeHow many due review cards per session, about 30 seconds each. Up to 30; anything over waits for a second pass.
Top up on light daysWhen fewer than 8 are due, offer extra practice on your weakest topics. Due reviews stay due-only; the extra is clearly separate so spacing isn't broken.
8Right-sized sessions. A session is whatever's actually due, capped at your chosen size (up to 30 cards, about 15 minutes), with a "keep going" pass on heavy days. On quiet days the optional top-up adds extra practice so it's still worth opening, without reviewing cards early and breaking their spacing. The controls sit under a Daily Review heading in Settings, so they're never confused with the standard quiz setup.
