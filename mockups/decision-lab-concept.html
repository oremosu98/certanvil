Decision Lab · design concept

The cloud-cert drill: read the constraint, kill the look-alikes, choose

Azure and AWS fundamentals exams have no PBQs. They test one thing under pressure: given a scenario with a buried constraint, pick the right service when three of four options look right. Decision Lab is the sibling to Sim Lab that drills exactly that. Same engine, honest framing: nothing is "simulated" or "hands-on", so it is never called a PBQ. One drill spans AZ-900 · AI-900 · SC-900 · AWS CLF-C02, and every future cloud cert.

Pick the best service
A finance team wants to know how much they'll save before moving their existing on-prem datacenter to Azure.
Constraint · compare on-prem vs cloud, before migrating
APricing CalculatorEstimates the cost of new Azure services you configure. It doesn't know your on-prem costs, so it can't show savings.✗
BTCO CalculatorCompares current on-prem spend against the Azure equivalent. That's the "how much will we save before moving" question.✓ Best
CCost ManagementMonitors spend on resources you already run in Azure. There's nothing to monitor before you migrate. (Your pick.)✗
DAzure AdvisorRecommends optimizations on deployed resources. Same problem: it needs live Azure usage first.✗
The tell
"Before moving" + "how much they'll save" means compare two worlds. Only the TCO Calculator compares on-prem against cloud. The other three need resources already running in Azure.
