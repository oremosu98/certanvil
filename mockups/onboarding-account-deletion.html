Delete your account?

Here is exactly what gets removed. Read it, then confirm by typing your email.

  • Your progress and history
    Every quiz, exam, and review you have done. Gone, not paused.
  • Your streak and readiness
    Your current 14 day streak and your readiness score reset to zero.
  • Your saved Pass Plan
    The study plan built around your exam date is deleted with everything else.
This cannot be undone. We do not keep a backup. To return later you would start a new account from scratch.
Your Pro subscription is billed by Apple, so deleting your account does not cancel it. To stop billing, cancel in Apple Settings, your name, Subscriptions. You can cancel any time.

This makes sure the delete is really you.
